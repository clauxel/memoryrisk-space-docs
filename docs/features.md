# Features - MemoryRisk

MemoryRisk scores BOM and server-list exposure to AI memory shortage risk, then turns the score into alternatives, budget sensitivity, supplier comparison, and purchase priority.

## BOM-level risk scoring

Score part, category, supplier, quantity, unit cost, lead weeks, memory type, and fallback availability.

## HBM and DDR5 exposure view

Separate AI accelerator memory pressure from server-grade DRAM, NAND, SSD, and bundle constraints.

## Budget sensitivity

Show how a memory price move, quote aging, or delay penalty changes exposed spend.

## Supplier comparison

Compare concentration, quote freshness, lead time, and alternate availability across vendors.

## Cloud fallback planning

Decide when cloud capacity is safer than waiting for constrained hardware.

## MSP client reporting

Turn shortage risk into a client-readable memo with purchase priority and timing.

## Pricing and Support

Plans cover watchlists, Team annual procurement reports, MSP client views, supplier comparison, and shortage-monitoring workflows.
