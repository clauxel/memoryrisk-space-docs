# Guides - MemoryRisk

MemoryRisk scores BOM and server-list exposure to AI memory shortage risk, then turns the score into alternatives, budget sensitivity, supplier comparison, and purchase priority.

## Prepare a BOM upload

Include part, category, supplier, quantity, unit cost, lead weeks, memory type, fallback, and notes.

## Read a shortage score

Focus on high-spend, high-dependency lines with weak substitutes or expiring allocations.

## Build a six-month plan

Tie purchase timing to quote validity, supplier allocation, cloud fallback, and budget risk.

## Pricing and Support

Plans cover watchlists, Team annual procurement reports, MSP client views, supplier comparison, and shortage-monitoring workflows.
