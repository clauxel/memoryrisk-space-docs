# How It Works - MemoryRisk

MemoryRisk scores BOM and server-list exposure to AI memory shortage risk, then turns the score into alternatives, budget sensitivity, supplier comparison, and purchase priority.

## Upload or enter the list

Start with part, category, supplier, quantity, unit cost, lead weeks, memory type, and fallback notes.

## Score exposure

Calculate risk from shortage pressure, supplier concentration, quote age, business dependency, and fallback realism.

## Review alternatives

Compare approved substitutes, split orders, cloud fallback, or delayed purchase options.

## Create the action plan

Prioritize pull-forward orders, watchlist items, finance approvals, and client reports.

## Pricing and Support

Plans cover watchlists, Team annual procurement reports, MSP client views, supplier comparison, and shortage-monitoring workflows.
