# Use Cases - MemoryRisk

MemoryRisk scores BOM and server-list exposure to AI memory shortage risk, then turns the score into alternatives, budget sensitivity, supplier comparison, and purchase priority.

## AI server procurement

Rank memory-heavy server lines before a purchase order is approved.

## MSP client report

Explain why a client should pull forward, split suppliers, or use cloud fallback.

## Finance budget review

Show exposed spend and scenario deltas if HBM or DDR5 pricing moves.

## Supplier negotiation prep

Enter quote age, lead time, and substitute readiness before negotiation.

## Pricing and Support

Plans cover watchlists, Team annual procurement reports, MSP client views, supplier comparison, and shortage-monitoring workflows.
